About

myHlist is a chrome extension that helps you organise your many nhentai doujinshi's and maximise your nutting experience without the need for logging in!

This extension allows you to freely keep track of the pages your on, go to any doujinshi at anytime with pages set, along with (not so many)other features!

Note: This extension is currently in early development, there are most likely some bugs present. But for now here are a few things to be aware of:

  • Tags are not yet avaliable to be searched in my list
  • The "add current with tags author etc." button only works on the front page of the doujinshi
  • I've tested the length capacity thoroughly, although I cannot garentee that you won't break the app with over 40 entries
  • Im currently storing all data within the local storage of the popup's domain (I know its bad), I will change that later on but for now I'd suggest you have a copy of your codes in a notepad or somthn
